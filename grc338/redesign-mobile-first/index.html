Gennaro’s Grill & Garden, a Northern Italian Restaurant is located in a delightful Old Victorian built on the Central Coast in 1904. The cuisine is classic Northern Italian with a Continental flair that includes fresh seafood, vegetarian and gluten-free dishes. All our pastas, breads and desserts are homemade and prepared in our kitchen daily.

We offer wines from the Central Coast, Napa Valley, Sonoma and of course Italy all chosen with passionate devotion that stems from Chef/Owner Gennaro’s extensive experience. We also have a full bar service Enjoy our fabulous Tapas with your favorite beverage!
