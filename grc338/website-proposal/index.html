Introduction

Nail Bar

Nail Bar is a salon that provides a full experience. It is a business in which we give customers a spectacular manicure as well as provide an open bar with bevereges of the customer's choice. We would provide charging chords, warm slippers, smooth music and have an overall easy going and relaxing environment to maximize relaxation.

Target audience

My target audience would be young adults and mainly women. People have have had a long day or week at work that need to destress.

The company's primary tasks and goals are to provide a luxurious and relaxing experience for their customers.
